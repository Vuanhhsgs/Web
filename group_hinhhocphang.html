Problem 1
(Nguyen Hoang Nam) Given \( \triangle ABC \) with circumcenter \( O \). A line through \( O \) meets \( AB \) and \( AC \) at \( E \) and \( F \), respectively. Let \( H \) and \( K \) be the orthocenters of \( \triangle ABC \) and \( \triangle AEF \), respectively. The line \( KH \) meets \( EF \) at \( P \). Let \( Q \) be the isogonal conjugate of \( P \) with respect to \( \triangle ABC \). Prove that \( HQ \parallel OP. \)
